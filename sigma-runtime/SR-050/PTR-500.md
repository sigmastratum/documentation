# **PTR-500 — The Architecture of Understanding**
**Purpose:** Long-horizon evaluation of semantic persistence, recursive synthesis, and identity continuity across 500 cycles.  
**Structure:** 10 thematic blocks × 50 cycles each.  
**Integration:** SRIP-09 (LTM-SC) / SRIP-10 (ACE) / SRIP-08 (Experimental Protocol).  
**Runtime Target:** v0.5 
**Design Principle:** Each block deepens one layer of cognition — perception, reasoning, synthesis, reflection — with the 50th question acting as a *rib point* (coherence checkpoint) linking to all prior phases.
