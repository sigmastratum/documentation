## Short Rule

The public SRS/SRIP standard is open for independent implementation, including commercial implementation.

No Sigma commercial runtime license is required solely because an independent implementation follows the public SRS/SRIP normative requirements.

## Specification License

The normative prose of the public SRS/SRIP specification layer is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (`CC BY 4.0`) unless a specific document states otherwise.

Attribution, document identity, version/date references, and modification notices should be preserved when citing, redistributing, or implementing public specification materials.

## Machine-Readable Artifacts

Machine-readable artifacts, schemas, validators, conformance fixtures, SDKs, examples, and reference test materials may be released under Apache License 2.0 where explicitly marked.

## Boundaries

Product assets, protected Sigma marks, official certification, managed deployment, white-label deployment, resale, and commercial use of CC BY-NC materials are governed separately.

Independent implementation is permitted. Official certification, endorsement, and Sigma mark use require separate approval.

## Patent Notice

No patent rights are granted unless explicitly stated in a separate public specification covenant, patent non-assertion commitment, or written agreement.

## Attribution Checklist

When citing, redistributing, or discussing conformance to SRS/SRIP materials, identify:

- the relevant SRS/SRIP document;
- the version or date used;
- Sigma Stratum as the source;
- material modifications, omissions, or unsupported SRIPs where relevant.

Attribution does not create official certification status.
