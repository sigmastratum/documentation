# Attribution Requirements

When using or referencing the Sigma Runtime Standard or associated materials:

You must:

- credit “Sigma Stratum Research Group”  
- include a link to the primary documentation site  
- include a link to the relevant SRIP or specification  

Examples of proper attribution:

> Based on the Sigma Runtime Standard 
>(© Sigma Stratum Research Group, CC BY-NC 4.0)  
> Source: https://wiki.sigmastratum.org/en/srs

Attribution is required for all non-commercial use.
