# Sigma Stratum Research Group (Team)

The Sigma Stratum Research Group (SSRG) is responsible for maintaining and
advancing the Sigma Runtime Standard (SRS) and the SRIP specification process.

The group includes contributors in:

- cognitive architectures  
- neurosymbolic systems  
- long-horizon reasoning  
- safety and recursive boundary research  
- interoperability design  
- documentation and publication  

Governance and contribution processes are defined in the subpages of this
section.
